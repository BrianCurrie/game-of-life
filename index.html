Conway's Game of Life

The Game of Life is played on a grid. Each cell on this grid is either alive or dead. A player determines the original state of the grid, after which four rules govern the subsequent generations:
1. A dead cell with exactly 3 living neighbors will come back to life
2. A living cell with exactly 2 or 3 living neighbors will stay alive
3. A living cell with less than 2 living neighbors will die
4. A living cell with 3 or more living neighbors will die
